Top International Travel Destinations

Below is a ranking of the top locations I've traveled to outside of the US

  1. Costa Rica
  2. Denmark
  3. Sweden
  4. Portugal
  5. Spain

Costa Rica

I went to Costa Rica in 2021 for an end of year project for school. I spent timing surfing, hiking, and learning about the local culture. The city that I visted is called Tamarindo, and it is home to some of the best surfing in Costa Rica. The image on the right is a photo of a lizard that I took outside of our house.

Denmark

Two summers ago I visted Denmark with my mom. I had the oppertunity to stay in the heart of Copenhagen. I visited museums, parks, and even went to a concert when I was there. Copenhagen is a beautiful city and the people are friendly.

Sweden

I visited Sweden during the same trip that I visted Denmark. It was a little "Europe Tour." Sweden was the second part of the trip, and we met up with some friends while we were there.

Portugal

I went to Portugal this past summer with my family. I went to the southern region of the country. The ocean and beaches are beautiful, with huge cliffs lining the blue ocean.

Spain

I went to Spain on the same trip that I went to Portugal. I visited Seville, in the southern region of the country. It was over 100 degress fahrenheit when I visited, however, the city was still enjoyable.
